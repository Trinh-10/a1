Project Idea

Overview

My project idea is an application that helps students find the right tutors. Education is one of the top priorities for the up-and-coming generation. That is why I feel this app should be created. The app will consist of profiles of tutors acting as advertisements on what subjects they teach with ratings shown to students. That way students can choose a tutor that’s right for them. With tutors and students using the app, they can both rate each other anonymously providing information for other tutors and students on whether they want to learn or teach from that person.

Motivation

This project idea would be useful to future generations education and helping students find the right tutors. I chose this idea because in my past high school years I couldn’t really find tutors who would help me to their fullest potential and weren’t that inclined to teach. Now that I realised how important it is receiving the best help, I want to create an app where students would have the opportunity to find the best tutors around.

Description

The application would be an educational service for students to help find tutors they want to benefit their education. Upon opening the app, it would ask whether the user is a student or tutor. The user would then create a profile depending on whether they are a student or a tutor. Tutoring profiles would need to be approved by administrators. Once students have completed their profile, they are able to use the app.

The app would allow students to search for what subject they need assistance in. A list of tutors would then load, showing their profile and their rating. Students are able to read the tutors profile and see their expertise, past experience and availability. They would also see reviews from other students on their previous tutoring sessions.

Some features the app would have are a chat a feature for students and tutors to contact each other. If a student has decided on a tutor, they can contact the tutor and schedule a time for a lesson. Students can also use the chat feature to ask the tutor questions. Another feature would be a file and document section where work can be stored. The rating and review feature would be available to both tutor and student after a session has been completed. Tutors are able to rate the student out of 5 stars and are able to leave a bit of a review on what the student is like. This is the same with the student. All of this would be anonymous and the rating and reviews can only be seen by students if a student wrote it and vice-versa. More features maybe added if idea is developed. This could include a meeting call for an online tutoring session to adapt to COVID-19.

Tools and Technology

The tools and technologies needed to create my project idea are programs such Swift, JavaScript and Xcode. Using a computer to run these programs, an app can be created with the required knowledge on how to operate them with coding and developing an app.

Skills Required

The skills required to develop this project idea is knowledge on front-end coding on developing and designing an app. This meaning expert knowledge on Swift, JavaScript, etc. Other skills that maybe required are problem-solving skills, communication with a team and leadership.

Outcome

If this app was to become successful, the outcome would be that students are better off with more knowledge especially those who struggle. Hopefully students learn a lot from their tutors and continue to use the app if they require assistance from other subjects. Even in the end, the students can create a tutoring profile and pass on the knowledge to the future generation. This app would have impacted those students beneficially helping them expand their knowledge.
